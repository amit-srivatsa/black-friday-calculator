The best way to use the deal

  • Point Black Friday at your soft, low-occupancy dates, not the weekends you already fill. Incremental headroom is the whole game.
  • Use length-of-stay or advance-purchase conditions so the discount pulls in net-new demand instead of rewarding guests who were booking anyway.
  • Black Friday is when late-fill and international demand peaks. That is exactly the demand least likely to cannibalise your full-price base.

Model notes

Net payout stacks discount, then commission, then Genius, matching how partners experience it. The no-deal baseline is rack minus commission, so Genius is treated as part of the promo stack. That sets break-even near the 38% incremental threshold seen in partner research. Loosen that assumption and break-even falls toward 30%.
