How to make it pay every time

  • A
    Aim the deal at your soft, low-occupancy dates, not the weekends you already fill. Empty-night headroom is what clears break-even.
  • B
    Add a length-of-stay or advance-purchase condition so the discount pulls in new demand instead of rewarding guests who were booking regardless.
  • C
    Black Friday is when late-fill and international demand peaks. That is the demand least likely to cannibalise your full-price base.

Net payout stacks discount, then commission, then Genius, mirroring how partners experience it. The no-deal baseline is rack minus commission, which places break-even near the 38% incremental threshold seen in partner research. Treat Genius as always-on and that bar falls toward 30%.
